Who is Jasmin Mitchell?

Front-end developer based in Connecticut, USA.

Hi there!
I am Jasmin, and I am an aspiring software engineer who loves a good challenge. When I'm not working on improving my coding skills, you can find me improving myself as a whole. I am big on self-development and I believe that when you focus on improving yourself, it has a positive affect on the people around you. I enjoy reading, working out, meditating, or doing literally anything outdoors (only during the summer though, unforunately it's cold on the east-coast right now). :( I'm a fun loving person who enjoys the little things that life has to offer.

I know that in combination with finding the right people to train me along with my strong work ethic, I would be an asset to any company in terms of the value I bring through my work and who I am as an indiviual. Thank you for reading these short paragraphs about me, I hope to talk to you soon, wherever you may be in this world!
